# Interview Preparation Timetable (Strict Execution Plan)

## Overview

-   **Duration:** 12 Weeks
-   **Daily Time:** 3.5--4 hours
-   **Goal:** Become interview-ready through execution, not just
    studying.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Daily Rules

1.  No YouTube unless completely blocked.
2.  No LeetCode discussions before solving.
3.  Every coding session is timed.
4.  Explain every solution aloud.
5.  Review mistakes the same day.
6.  Maintain a mistake journal.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Mistake Journal

Every day write:

-   Problems I couldn't solve
-   Concepts I forgot
-   Bugs I wrote
-   Questions I couldn't answer verbally

If a topic appears **3 times**, make it tomorrow's first study topic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Weekly Targets

-   8--10 Medium problems
-   2 Hard problems
-   1 Full DSA mock
-   1 Backend mock
-   1 System Design discussion
-   Theory revision every week

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# End Goal

By the end of two weeks you should comfortably:

-   Solve medium problems in 25--35 minutes.
-   Explain OS, DB, and Networking without notes.
-   Handle backend discussions confidently.
-   Finish complete interview rounds without freezing.
